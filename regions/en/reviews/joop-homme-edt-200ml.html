JOOP! Homme - Eau de Toilette for Men Review

Not sure if I'll repurchase at £28, pricey.

Alright, so I decided to give JOOP! Homme a go, and here's my honest take on it. This woody fragrance for men comes with a mix of orange blossom, tonka bean accord, and honey. It's priced at £28, which I found a bit on the higher side, but let's see if it's worth it.

First off, the scent itself is quite nice. It's not too overpowering, which is a plus in my book. The orange blossom adds a fresh touch, and the tonka bean accord gives it a unique depth. The honey note is there, but it's not as strong as I expected, which is fine by me.

Now, let's talk about the features. The longevity is medium, which means you might need to reapply during the day. The bottle is sleek and looks good on the shelf, but it's not the most practical design for travel. The scent profile is definitely woody, with a good balance of sweet and fresh notes.

Pros:

  • Nice, balanced scent
  • Not overpowering
  • Orange blossom adds freshness
  • Sleek bottle design

Cons:

  • Pricey at £28
  • Medium longevity might require reapplication
  • Not the most practical bottle for travel

In the end, JOOP! Homme is a solid choice if you're after a woody fragrance with a fresh twist. The scent is nice, but the price and the medium longevity might make you think twice. If you're willing to spend a bit more and don't mind reapplying, it's definitely worth a try.
