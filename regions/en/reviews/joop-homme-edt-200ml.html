JOOP! Homme - Eau de Toilette for Men Review

Been searching for a decent woody scent, this one's alright.

So, I've been on the hunt for a new woody fragrance that won't break the bank, and I stumbled upon JOOP! Homme. It's priced at £28, which is pretty reasonable for a 125ml bottle. Let's dive into what it's all about.

JOOP! Homme is an Eau de Toilette for men that promises a woody fragrance with notes of orange blossom, tonka bean accord, and honey. It's meant to have medium longevity, which I'll get into a bit later.

Features

  • Woody fragrance with notes of orange blossom, tonka bean accord, and honey
  • Medium longevity
  • 125ml bottle
  • £28 price point

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Great value for money
  • The scent is pleasant and not overpowering
  • The orange blossom adds a nice twist to the woody base

Cons

  • The longevity could be better - it lasts about 4-5 hours on me
  • The honey note can be a bit too sweet for some

Verdict

Overall, JOOP! Homme is a solid choice if you're after a woody fragrance that won't break the bank. The scent is pleasant, and the orange blossom adds a nice twist to the mix. However, the longevity could be better, and the honey note might be a bit too sweet for some. If you're looking for a budget-friendly woody scent, give it a go.
