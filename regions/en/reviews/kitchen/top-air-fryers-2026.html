Buying Guide

When choosing an air fryer, consider the following factors to ensure you select the best model for your needs:

1. Capacity

The capacity of an air fryer is crucial, especially if you're cooking for a family. Air fryers typically range from 2 to 6 litres. A larger capacity means you can cook more food at once.

2. Power

Higher wattage generally means faster cooking times. Most air fryers range from 1200W to 1750W. Consider your energy consumption needs and how quickly you want your food to cook.

3. Features

Modern air fryers come with various features such as digital touchscreens, preset cooking programs, and smart app control. Choose features that align with your cooking habits and preferences.

4. Ease of Use and Cleaning

Look for air fryers with non-stick baskets and dishwasher-safe parts for easy cleaning. A user-friendly interface and clear instructions can also enhance your cooking experience.

5. Price

Air fryers come in a wide price range. Determine your budget and find a model that offers the best value for your money. Consider the long-term benefits and durability of the appliance.

6. Brand and Reviews

Trusted brands often provide better quality and customer support. Reading customer reviews can give you insights into real-world performance and reliability.

Conclusion

Choosing the right air fryer can transform your cooking experience, making it healthier and more convenient. Whether you're looking for a budget-friendly option or a high-end model with smart features, our list of the top 10 best air fryers in the UK for 2026 has something for everyone. Happy cooking!
