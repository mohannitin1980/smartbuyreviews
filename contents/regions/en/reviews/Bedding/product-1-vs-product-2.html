NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream (500ml) Review

48H Face Moisturiser for Face, Body and Hands with Vitamin E and Jojoba Oil

Price: £5.50

[IMAGE_1]

Introduction

The NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream is a versatile product designed to hydrate and nourish your skin across face, body, and hands. With a 500ml bottle, it promises 48-hour moisture, enriched with Vitamin E and Jojoba Oil. But does it live up to its claims? Here’s an honest review based on my experience with the product.

Product Description

The NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream is marketed as a highly effective, revitalising, and moisturising cream that leaves your skin soft and supple for up to 48 hours. It's an all-purpose moisturiser designed for face, body, and hands, helping you save space on your bathroom shelves. The formula contains Vitamin E and Jojoba Oil, promising quick absorption and deep moisturisation. The application is simple: massage into the skin post-shower for maximum benefit, and take extra care to avoid the eye area when using it as a face moisturiser.

First Impressions

Upon receiving the NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream, I was immediately impressed by the size of the bottle. At 500ml, it's a generous amount that promises to last for a while. The packaging is simple yet effective, with a flip-top cap that's easy to use. The cream itself has a mild, pleasant scent that isn't overpowering, which is a big plus for those who are sensitive to strong fragrances.

Application and Texture

The cream has a smooth, creamy texture that spreads easily on the skin. It's not too thick, which makes it ideal for those who prefer a lighter feel. The claim of quick absorption is accurate; the cream sinks into the skin within a minute or two, leaving no greasy residue. This is particularly beneficial for daytime use, as it doesn't leave your skin feeling sticky or heavy.

Moisturising Power

One of the main selling points of this product is its 48-hour moisturising claim. In my experience, the cream does a good job of keeping my skin hydrated for the day, but I found that I needed to reapply it after about 24 hours to maintain the same level of moisture. This is not necessarily a deal-breaker, as the cream still provides excellent hydration throughout the day, but it doesn't quite live up to the 48-hour promise.

Versatility

The NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream is indeed versatile. I used it on my face, body, and hands, and it performed well in all areas. On my face, it provided a good base for makeup without causing any breakouts, which is a relief for someone with combination skin. On my body, it helped to keep my skin soft and smooth, especially after a shower. For my hands, which tend to get dry from frequent washing, the cream provided much-needed relief and kept them from feeling tight and dry.

Ingredients

The inclusion of Vitamin E and Jojoba Oil is a nice touch. Vitamin E is known for its antioxidant properties, which can help protect the skin from environmental damage. Jojoba Oil, on the other hand, is similar to the skin's natural oils, making it an excellent moisturiser. The fact that this product is vegan is also a big plus for those who prefer cruelty-free skincare.

Price and Value

Priced at £5.50, the NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream is very affordable, especially considering the large 500ml bottle. The value for money is excellent, as you get a versatile product that can be used on your face, body, and hands. If you're looking for a budget-friendly moisturiser that performs well, this is a great option.

Final Thoughts

Overall, the NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream is a solid choice for those seeking a versatile, affordable, and effective moisturiser. It doesn't quite meet the 48-hour moisture claim, but it still provides excellent hydration throughout the day. The quick absorption, pleasant scent, and inclusion of beneficial ingredients like Vitamin E and Jojoba Oil make it a product worth considering. If you're looking for a budget-friendly, all-purpose moisturiser that won't disappoint, the NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream is definitely worth a try.

Honest Review

The NIVEA Creme Tin is a staple in many households, and it's easy to see why. With a generous 400ml capacity, this tin offers a lot of product for the price, which is set at a very reasonable £4.45. The cream promises intensive protective care, and it largely delivers on this promise, making it a versatile and reliable choice for those seeking a moisturiser that can be used on the face, hands, and body.

Upon opening the tin, you're greeted with NIVEA's iconic scent, which is a blend of floral and slightly medicinal notes. On one hand, it provides a deep level of hydration that can transform dry, rough skin into something much softer and more supple. On the other hand, the heaviness of the cream can be a bit much for some skin types, particularly those with oily or combination skin.

One of the standout features of the NIVEA Creme Tin is its versatility. It's marketed as an all-purpose cream, and it truly lives up to this claim. If you're in the market for a moisturiser that can do it all and won't break the bank, the NIVEA Creme Tin is definitely worth considering.
