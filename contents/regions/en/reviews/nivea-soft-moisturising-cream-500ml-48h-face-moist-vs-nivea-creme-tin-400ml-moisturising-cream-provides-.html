Overview

The NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream is a versatile product designed to hydrate and nourish the skin on your face, body, and hands. With a large 500ml bottle, it promises up to 48 hours of moisturization, enriched with Vitamin E and Jojoba Oil. Priced between £4.50 and £13.00, depending on the retailer, it aims to be an affordable yet effective skincare solution.

Performance

Upon first use, the NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream feels light and non-greasy, which is a significant advantage for those who dislike heavy lotions. The cream absorbs quickly into the skin, leaving a subtle, pleasant scent that isn't overpowering. It's true to its claim of quick absorption, which is a major plus for busy mornings or when you need to get dressed right after application.

The moisturization is evident almost immediately, with the skin feeling softer and more supple. However, the promise of 48 hours of moisture is somewhat ambitious. While the cream does provide lasting hydration, I found that reapplication after about 12 to 18 hours was necessary to maintain the desired level of moisture, especially on drier areas like the elbows and knees.

Versatility

One of the standout features of this product is its versatility. It's designed to be used on the face, body, and hands, which is a great space-saver for those with limited bathroom storage. As a hand lotion, it works wonderfully, keeping my hands soft and smooth without any sticky residue. On the body, it's equally effective, providing a nice layer of moisture without feeling heavy.

Using it as a face moisturiser, however, requires a bit more caution. The formula is suitable for normal to dry skin types, but those with sensitive or acne-prone skin might find it a bit too rich. The instructions advise avoiding the eye area, which is crucial as the cream can be slightly irritating if it gets too close to the eyes.

Ingredients

The inclusion of Vitamin E and Jojoba Oil is a positive aspect of this cream. Vitamin E is known for its antioxidant properties, which help to protect the skin from environmental damage, while Jojoba Oil is excellent for hydrating and balancing the skin's natural oils. The fact that it's a vegan formula is also a plus for those who prefer plant-based skincare products.

Value for Money

Priced between £4.50 and £13.00, the NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream offers good value for money, especially considering the large 500ml bottle. This size lasts for a long time, even with daily use on the face, body, and hands. The price range is reasonable, making it accessible to a wide range of consumers.

Final Thoughts

Overall, the NIVEA Soft Moisturising Cream is a solid choice for those looking for an affordable, multi-purpose moisturiser. It's quick-absorbing, non-greasy, and leaves the skin feeling soft and hydrated. While the 48-hour hydration claim might be a bit of a stretch, it still offers significant moisture that lasts a good part of the day.

If you have sensitive or acne-prone skin, you might want to test it on a small area first or consider a different product specifically designed for your skin type. However, for most people, this cream is a reliable and versatile addition to their skincare routine.
